I'm a Computer Science and Engineering graduate from Politecnico di Milano (DEIB), 
with a focus on football analytics and machine learning.

My master's thesis investigated the expected goals (xG) metric in professional soccer, 
building match outcome prediction models using StatsBomb event data and LightGBM classifiers. 

I also developed a football scouting system in Python that identifies player profiles 
across the five major European leagues using composite scoring and style metrics.

I am passionate about applying data science to football and actively looking for 
opportunities in the sports analytics industry.
