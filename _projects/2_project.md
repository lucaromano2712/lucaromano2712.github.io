A Python-based scouting system built on StatsBomb open data that identifies 
player profiles across La Liga, Premier League, Bundesliga, Serie A, and Ligue 1.

The system implements a `scout()` function with composite scoring based on 
style, gap, and role-fit metrics, validated against real 2016 summer transfers.
