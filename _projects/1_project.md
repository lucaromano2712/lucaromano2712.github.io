A critical analysis of the expected goals (xG) metric in professional soccer, 
investigating its effectiveness for match outcome prediction.

Built using **StatsBomb** open event data across 3,464 matches from the five 
major European leagues, with **LightGBM** classifiers and a GroupKFold 
cross-validation pipeline.

Key contributions:
- Comparison of shot-based, freeze-frame, and combined xG models
- SMOTE ablation and permutation tests for statistical validation
- SHAP analysis for model interpretability

Submitted to the **International Journal of Sports Science & Coaching**.
