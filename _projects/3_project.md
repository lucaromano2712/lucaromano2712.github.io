A machine learning pipeline for predicting Serie A match outcomes and evaluating 
bookmaker odds to identify value bets.

Historical match data and statistical features were used to train classification 
models, whose probability estimates were then compared against bookmaker odds 
to assess market efficiency and potential edges.
